Minestrone Soup

  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage, about half a head
  • 2-3 carrots, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely cut
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 small zuccini, diced
  • 1 small yellow squash, diced
  • 6 cups beef or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp Better than Bouillan, veggie or beef
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • bucatini or spaghetti noodles
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • Grated or shaved parmesan for serving
  • Half a lemon
  • Hot sauce for serving, preferrably Tabasco

Prep vegetables and set aside. Heat Dutch oven or large soup pot over medium-high heat with several glugs of olive oil. Once hot, add onion, cabbage and carrots. Stir, adjust temperature of cooking too hot. Let cook for 3-5 minutes until cabbage is soft. Add garlic, oregano, red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds or just until you smell the garlic. Season with salt and pepper.


Add tomatoes, zuccini, squash, stock, bouillan and 1 cup of water. Stir to combine and bring to a simmer, about 10 minutes. Check salt, add more if needed. Add rinsed beans, kale, and bucatini -- broken into 2-3 inch pieces. Cook until pasta is al dente.


Serve with parmesan, squeeze and hot sauce (optional). Enjoy!
