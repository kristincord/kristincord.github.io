Minestrone Soup

  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage, about half a head
  • 2-3 carrots
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 small zuccini
  • 1 small yellow squash
  • 6 cups beef or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp better than bouillan
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • Grated or shaved parmesan for serving
  • Hot sauce for serving, preferrably Tabasco
