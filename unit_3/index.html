Minestrone Soup

  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 cups shredded cabbage, about half a head
  • 2-3 carrots, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, finely cut
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 small zuccini, diced
  • 1 small yellow squash, diced
  • 6 cups beef or vegetable stock
  • 1 tsp Better than Bouillan, veggie or beef
  • 2 cups chopped kale
  • bucatini or spaghetti noodles
  • 1 can garbanzo beans
  • Grated or shaved parmesan for serving
  • Hot sauce for serving, preferrably Tabasco

Prep vegetables and set aside. Heat Dutch oven or soup pot over medium-high heat with several glugs of olive oil. Once hot, add onion, cabbage and carrots. Stir, adjust temperature of cooking too hot. Let cook for 3-5 minutes until cabbage is soft. Add garlic, oregano, red pepper flakes and cook for 30 seconds.
